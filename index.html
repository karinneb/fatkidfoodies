The fat kids aim to try all the best restaurants in Seattle!

Eric and Karinne love food, but also can't eat like fat kids all the time and not be obese. So, they get together every now and then and try to hit as many new restaurants in one day as they can. And then food coma. Thus, it is known as "fat kid day!"

This website is for us to keep track of all of the places we've been -- and more importantly, recommend.


How do we do this?

Eric LOVES to cook, and follows a ton of blogs. Karinne frequently falls down the rabbit hole of Yelp. We share a google spreadsheet to track it all!
